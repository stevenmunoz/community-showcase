FocusFlow

Side Project
by Lena Mori

A minimal Pomodoro app that adapts session lengths based on your energy levels throughout the day. Built with vanilla JS and localStorage.

Mental ModelParkinson's Law — work expands to fill the time available. So I let the timer shrink when energy is high.

Ship the ugly version first. My v1 was embarrassing, but it taught me what users actually wanted.

SupplyPulse

Company
by Marco Delgado

Internal dashboard that visualizes supply-chain bottlenecks in real time. Reduced our team's response time by 40%.

Mental ModelTheory of Constraints — find the one bottleneck, fix it, then find the next one. Don't optimize everything at once.

Talk to the warehouse team before writing a single line of code. Domain knowledge > technical skill.

TinyMVP

Prototype
by Aisha Patel

A no-code landing page generator that creates a waitlist + Stripe checkout in under 5 minutes. Still in prototype, 200 signups so far.

Mental ModelThe Mom Test — don't ask "would you use this?" Ask "how do you solve this problem today?" and watch what they do.

Charge from day one, even $1. It filters out people who'd never pay and gives you real signal.

RetroBoard

Side Project
by Jonas Ekström

A real-time retrospective tool for remote teams with anonymous voting, action tracking, and Slack integration.

Mental ModelJobs to Be Done — people don't want a retro tool, they want their team to improve every sprint. Design for the outcome.

Build for your own team first. If it solves your problem well, there are 1,000 teams with the same one.
